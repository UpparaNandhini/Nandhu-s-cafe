Our Story

Crafted with Care,
Served with Soul

Nandhu's Cafe began in a tiny 200 sq ft space with a single La Marzocco and an obsessive belief that coffee could be both exceptional and comforting. A decade later, we still pull every shot by hand.

We partner directly with small-scale farmers in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Sumatra, paying above fair-trade prices so that the care put into growing the beans matches the care we put into brewing them.

Our pastries are baked fresh each morning. Our space is designed for slow mornings and long conversations. Come as you are — stay as long as you like.

When We're Open

Opening Hours

We open early so you can start your morning right. Last orders are 20 minutes before closing. The kitchen stops taking food orders at 5 pm.
